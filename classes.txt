Class: Dog

Attributes:

name (string)
breed (string)
height (integer)
known_commands (array)
standing (boolean)

Methods:

rename (changes name)
show_breed (displays breed)
show_height (displays height in inches)
learn_trick (adds new_trick to end of known_commands array)
sit (changes standing to "false")



Class: Boat

Attributes:

length (integer)
color (string)
bunks (integer)
crew_members (array)
crew_titles (hash)

Methods:

show_length (displays length of boat)
repaint (changes color of boat)
add_sleeping (adds to bunks)
fire_crew (removes a member from array crew_members)
display_captain (displays the name of the captain from hash crew_titles)
