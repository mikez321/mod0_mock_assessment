Top down design task:  MAKING A SANDWICH

1 Prepare a sandwich building station
  1.1 Find an area to prepare sandwich
  1.2 Clear the area
  1.3 Get a knife
    1.3.1 Open knife drawer
    1.3.2 Grab chef's knife
    1.3.3 Hone knife
      1.3.3.1 Grab honing steel from drawer
      1.3.3.2 Draw knife blade across steel
      1.3.3.3 Put away honing steel
    1.3.4 Close knife drawer
  1.4 Get cutting board
  1.5 Place items in prep area
    1.5.1 Knoll cutting board and knife

2 Get sandwich supplies
  2.1 Get ham
    2.1.1 Open meat drawer
      2.1.1.1 open fridge
      2.1.1.2 open meat drawer
    2.1.2 Grab ham
    2.1.3 Place ham in prep area
    2.1.4 Close fridge
      2.1.4.1 Close meat drawer
      2.1.4.2 Close fridge
  2.2 Get cheese
    2.2.1 Open fridge
    2.2.2 Grab cheese
    2.2.3 Place cheese in prep area
    2.2.4 Close fridge
  2.3 Get veggies
    2.3.1 Open veggie drawer
      2.3.1.1 Open fridge
      2.3.1.2 Open veggie drawer
    2.3.2 Grab Lettuce
    2.3.3 Place Lettuce in prep area
    2.3.4 Grab Tomato
    2.3.5 Place tomato in prep area
    2.3.6 Close fridge
      2.3.6.1 Close veggie drawer
      2.3.6.2 Close fridge
  2.4 Get bread
    2.4.1 open fridge
    2.4.2 grab bread
    2.4.3 place bread in prep area
    2.4.4 close fridge
  2.5 Get add-ons
    2.5.1 open fridge
    2.5.2 grab dijon_mustard
    2.5.3 place dijon_mustard in prep area
    2.5.4 grab mayo
    2.5.5 place mayo in prep area
    2.5.6 Close fridge
  2.6 Get plate
    2.6.1 open plate cabinet
    2.6.2 get plate
    2.6.3 put plate on counter
    2.6.4 close plate cabinet


3 Assemble the sandwich
  3.1 bread
    3.1.1 Open bread
    3.1.2 Grab two slices
      3.1.2.1 Put one slice on plate
      3.1.2.2 Put one slice aside
    3.1.3 Close bread
  3.2 meat
    3.2.1 Open ham
    3.2.2 Grab 3 slices of ham
    3.2.3 Place the ham on the bread
    3.2.4 close ham
  3.3 veggies
    3.3.1 Add Lettuce
      3.3.1.1 Open lettuce container
      3.3.1.2 Break off a good leaf
      3.3.1.3 Wash the leaf
        3.3.1.3.1 Turn on sink water
        3.3.1.3.2 clean leaf in water
        3.3.1.3.3 Turn off sink water
        3.3.1.3.4 Dry leaf with paper towel
          3.3.1.3.4.1 Get paper towel
      3.3.1.4 Place lettuce on sandwich
    3.3.2 Add Tomato
      3.3.2.1 Cut tomato_slice
        3.3.2.1.1 use kife and cutting board to cut end off Tomato
        3.3.2.1.2 discard end
        3.3.2.1.3 cut a slice of Tomato
      3.3.2.2 Place tomato_slice on sandwich
  3.4 extras
    3.4.1 Add mayo
      3.4.1.1 grab mayo
      3.4.1.2 open mayo
        3.4.1.2.1 unscrew lid
        3.4.1.2.2 place lid in prep area
      3.4.1.3 apply mayo to sandwich with knife
      3.4.1.4 close mayo
        3.4.1.4.1 pick up lid
        3.4.1.4.2 screw lid on mayo
    3.4.2 add dijon_mustard
      3.4.2.1 grab dijon_mustard
      3.4.2.2 shake dijon_mustard
      3.4.2.3 squeeze dijon_mustard onto sandwich
  3.5 bread_cap
    3.5.1 pick up bread_cap
    3.5.2 place on top of sandwich
